Publications

An Efficient Multi-Rater Setup Towards Personalized and Diversified Medical Image Segmentation

IEEE/CVF Winter Conference on Applications of Computer Vision (WACV 2026) Accepted

Sajed Almorsy, Ahmed Khalafallah, Marwan Torki

We propose P-Diverse, a novel two-stage framework for multi-rater medical image segmentation that minimizes annotation needs. Stage-I trains a modified nnU-Net with expert-specific embeddings throughout the network stages, generating personalized segmentations using as low as one annotation per scan. Stage-II synthesizes missing annotations and trains a diversification model that captures multi-rater variability. Evaluated on NPC and QUBIQ2021 datasets, P-Diverse achieves state-of-the-art performance while significantly reducing clinical annotation burdens.
